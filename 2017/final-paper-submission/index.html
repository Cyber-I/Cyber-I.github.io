Final Paper Submission

Instructions for the Final Paper and Copyright Submission to IEEE Conference Publishing Service:

  1.  Please prepare your camera-ready copy carefully by taking the comments of the reviewers.
  2.  Please follow the IEEE format to prepare your final manuscript, and submit the final manuscript file (also the copyright form) to the conference proceeding submission link specified by IEEE CPS. (The author kit and submission link will be available after 27 th May.)
  3.  The paper must comply with the following page limit and purchasing extra pages if needed Regular Paper: 8 pages, up to 2 extra pages at 50USD per extra page. Special Session/Workshop Paper: 6 pages, up to 2 extra pages at 50USD per extra page. Poster Paper: 4 pages, up to 2 extra pages at 50USD per extra page.
  4.  You may be contacted by an IEEE CPS editorial staff about detailed final manuscript instructions.
  5.  A hyperlink to IEEE Proceedings Author Kit will also be provided here in few days.
